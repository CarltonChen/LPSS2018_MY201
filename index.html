Big Data: Data Analytics for Business and Beyond

Long ago, big data was a thick screen, I was here, mainframe computing was there.
从前吧，大数据是一块厚厚的屏幕，我在这头，大型主机在那头。

And now, big data was a thin smart-phone, I am here, cloud computing is there.
而现在，大数据是一部薄薄的手机，我在这头，云计算网在那头。

In the future, big data will be a tiny particle, I will be here, quantum computing will be there.
到将来，大数据是一个小小的粒子，我在这头，量子计算在那头。

-- Yazhen Wang /王亚珍, 18 May 2017
